Details:

On July 1, 2023, Ontario's population reached 15,608,369. Over the 12-months, the provincial population grew by 463,363 residents, constituting an annual 3.1% increase. This application is intended for home- buyers, retail analysts and interested parties to look at the housing prices and localities of for sale homes in Ontario across all major cities via listing statistics and an interactive map. All data presented is processed >6 hours.

AWS Architecture:


Use Cases:

  • Analyze the geolocation of listings in Ontario with listing data updated on a > 6 hr basis.
  • Assess address, price, and listing of home relative to locality.
  • Examine housing metric for specific locality including lowest (listings >$100,000 in value), highest and average listing prices.
  • The source system has the largest repositories in Ontario of house listings. This application allows you to see listings data of localities in geospatial and tabular formats.

Limitations:

  • Incorrect geospatial labelling of data could result in inaccurate readings of data. For example, a listing located in Mississauga could incorrectly be associated with a listing in Brampton.
  • Fluctuation of listings data, dependent on how many listing data is pulled from source system.
  • Possibility of missing values (i.e., address, postal codes, prices, etc.).
  • Listing prices are not fixed as prices may be subject to bidding.
  • Listing may be expired.
